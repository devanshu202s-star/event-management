About Culture Fusion

Culture Fusion is a grand cultural event where people from different backgrounds come together to celebrate traditions, art, music and food from around the world. This festival promotes unity, creativity and cultural diversity. Students showcase dance performances, fashion shows, international cuisine, music concerts and cultural exhibitions.

Main Events

Global Dance Show

Dance performances from Bollywood, Hip Hop, Salsa and traditional styles.

International Food Festival

Enjoy delicious food from India, Korea, Italy, Japan and many other cultures.

Cultural Fashion Walk

Students showcase traditional and modern fashion from different cultures.
