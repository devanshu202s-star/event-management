Festival Events

Culture Fusion offers a variety of exciting activities celebrating cultures from around the world. Students and performers participate in dance, music, fashion, and culinary experiences.

Global Dance Show

Watch amazing dance performances including Bollywood, Hip-Hop, Salsa, and traditional cultural dances from different countries.

International Food Festival

Enjoy delicious food from different cultures including Indian, Italian, Korean, Japanese, and Mexican cuisines.

Cultural Fashion Walk

Participants showcase traditional outfits and modern cultural fashion representing their heritage.

Live Music Concert

Enjoy live performances from college bands, singers, and instrumental artists.

Art & Craft Exhibition

Students display handmade crafts, paintings, and creative artworks inspired by different cultures.

Cultural Games

Participate in fun games and competitions inspired by traditional cultural activities.
